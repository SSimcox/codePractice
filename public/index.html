Challenge

Ash Ketchup has got stuck in a maze. He has limited vision and is scared of getting lost in the maze. Luckily a kind-hearted specter has taken pity on him and decided to tell him how to get out. The problem is that the specter can only communicate via the console. Read in the console input (u, d, l, r, f = no more commands) and report back to Ash what direct to turn ("Go Up", "Go Down", "Go Right", "Go Left") to help him escape the maze.
